Where to Eat in Espoo

1. Bodomin Kartano

A historic manor-turned-restaurant set by Lake Bodom, Bodomin Kartano serves refined Finnish classics using seasonal, local ingredients. Expect elegant presentations and sweeping water views—perfect for a special-occasion dinner.

2. Bistro O mat Tapiola

Cozy and contemporary, this neighborhood bistro in Tapiola offers a concise menu of European-inspired comfort dishes. Fresh salads, house-made pastas, and daily specials make it ideal for a relaxed lunch or early-evening meal.

3. Bangkok 9 – Iso Omena

Authentic Thai favorites like pad thai, green curry, and tom yam soup are served in a vibrant, family-friendly setting. Generous portions and bold flavors have made Bangkok 9 a local go-to for Southeast Asian cuisine.

4. Ravilla – Vermo Areena

Perched alongside the Vermo racetrack, Ravilla highlights Finnish seasonal produce—from wild mushroom risottos to reindeer carpaccio. Panoramic windows overlook the river, offering a serene backdrop to your meal.

5. Kylä

Bib Gourmand–recognized Kylä specializes in New Nordic tasting menus crafted from local farm and forest ingredients. Intimate dining rooms and artful plating create a modern, immersive Finnish culinary experience.

6. Green Sushi

Located in Kauppakeskus Liila, Green Sushi Suomenoja offers an all-you-can-eat Asian buffet with over 35 sushi varieties, hot dishes, Vietnamese pho, a fresh salad bar, and teppanyaki stations for a complete sushi feast.

7. Farang Espoo

Farang brings South-East Asian flavors to life with slow-cooked, aromatic dishes in a stylish, minimalist setting. Their chefs explore bold ingredient pairings and traditional techniques for an unforgettable, communal dining experience.

8. Mezza Restaurant & Cafe

Mediterranean flavors take center stage here, with mezze platters, fresh seafood dishes, and artisanal breads. Light, airy interiors and a sunny terrace make it a delightful spot for brunch or evening dining.

9. Juuri

Known for its “sapas” (Finnish tapas), Juuri elevates local ingredients—think beetroot carpaccio, smoked vendace tartines, and lingonberry-infused desserts. Warm hospitality completes the modern yet cozy ambiance.

10. Ravintola Ragu

Ragu blends Italian classics with Nordic purity, offering handmade pastas, wood-fired breads, and thoughtfully curated wine pairings. Warm, rustic décor complements its ever-changing seasonal menu rooted in local produce.

Espoo's Best Places to Stay

1. Hotel Matts

A stylish boutique hotel in Otaniemi, Hotel Matts offers sleek, Scandinavian-designed rooms, a cozy in-house café, and easy tram access to central Espoo and Helsinki.

2. Hotel Hanasaari

Set on its own island peninsula, this waterfront resort features panoramic Gulf of Finland views, a full spa, indoor pool, and fine dining—ideal for a luxurious escape by the sea.

3. Hotelli Korpilampi

Nestled beside a serene lake, Korpilampi provides spacious rooms, lakeside sauna access, an indoor pool, and forest-view walking trails—perfect for blending relaxation with nature.

4. Original Sokos Hotel Tapiola Garden

In the heart of Tapiola, this modern city-center hotel features bright rooms, an acclaimed breakfast buffet, and is steps from shopping, restaurants, and public transport hubs.

5. Scandic Espoo

A contemporary business hotel near Leppävaara’s Sello Shopping Centre, offering soundproofed rooms, a 24/7 fitness center, and easy rail connections to Espoo’s top attractions.

6. Backby Manor

Housed in a 19th-century manor on Espoo’s outskirts, it delivers charming, individually styled rooms, gourmet dining, and extensive spa and garden facilities for a boutique countryside stay.

7. Noli Otaniemi

Located in the tech-campus district, Noli Otaniemi offers compact, design-forward studios with kitchenette, high-speed WiFi, and communal lounges—ideal for longer stays or work-from-hotel setups.

8. Forenom Aparthotel Espoo Leppävaara

This budget-friendly aparthotel provides self-contained studios and one-bedroom apartments with kitchens, laundry facilities, and flexible check-in—great for families or extended visits.

9. Hiisi Homes Espoo Center

Situated steps from Espoo’s central train station, Hiisi Homes offers modern apartment-style suites with private sauna options, fully equipped kitchens, and in-building grocery services.

10. Kattilajärvi Camping Area

For a true wilderness retreat, pitch a tent or rent a simple lakeside cabin at Kattilajärvi in Nuuksio National Park—complete with campfire shelters, dry toilets, and swimming access.
